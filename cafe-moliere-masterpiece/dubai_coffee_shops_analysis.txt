DUBAI COFFEE SHOP WEBSITE ANALYSIS & BENCHMARKING

Below is an analysis of the digital presence, tech stacks, and design philosophies of Dubai's most prominent specialty coffee establishments.

---
1. NIGHTJAR COFFEE ROASTERS (Alserkal Avenue)
   Website: nightjar.coffee
   Tech Stack: Shopify, Tailwind CSS, Flickity.
   Design: "Indie/Editorial Counterculture". Bold black and yellow palette. Industrial-chic imagery.
   Key Elements:
   - Scrolling Marquee announcement bars.
   - Massive, heavy sans-serif typography (impactful headings).
   - High-contrast e-commerce layout that feels like a streetwear brand.
   - Use of grainy, raw lifestyle photography.

2. % ARABICA UAE (Multiple Locations)
   Website: arabicacoffee.ae
   Tech Stack: WordPress, Elementor (Custom Minimalism).
   Design: "Japanese Minimalism". Clean, clinical, ultra-premium.
   Key Elements:
   - Full-screen high-quality hero video of manual brewing.
   - Extreme white space (less is more).
   - Subtle, hidden navigation to emphasize visual storytelling.
   - Iconic logo placement as a central design architectural element.

3. STOMPING GROUNDS (Jumeirah)
   Website: stompinggrounds.me
   Tech Stack: WordPress / Custom Heritage Build.
   Design: "Classic Heritage / Rustic Villa". Warm, inviting, and nostalgic.
   Key Elements:
   - Parchment/Cream backgrounds (#efefef) instead of sterile white.
   - Serif typography for an "award-winning" established feel.
   - Heavy focus on location photography (The Villa).
   - Menu-centric UX designed for neighborhood regulars.

4. RAW COFFEE COMPANY (Al Quoz)
   Website: rawcoffeecompany.com
   Tech Stack: Shopify, Headless Next.js elements.
   Design: "Industrial Workshop". Functional, informative, and ethical.
   Key Elements:
   - Educational iconography for coffee processing.
   - Crimson and Slate Grey color accents.
   - Prominent subscription management UI.
   - Warehouse-style aesthetics that match their Al Quoz physical space.

5. THE ESPRESSO LAB (D3 / Jumeirah)
   Website: theespressolab.com
   Tech Stack: Shopify.
   Design: "The Scientific Boutique". Precise, curated, and exclusive.
   Key Elements:
   - Boutique-style product grids (feels like luxury fashion).
   - Minimalist grid systems with strict mathematical alignment.
   - Quick-access seasonal bean filtering.
   - High-end curated packaging photography.
